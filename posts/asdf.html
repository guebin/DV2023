3. map(function, list)

A. lambda

- 예시1: \(x \to x+2\)

f = lambda x: x+2 
f(3)
5

- 예시2: \(x,y \to x+y\)

f = lambda x,y: x+y 
f(3,5)
8

- 예시3: ‘2023-09’ \(\to\) 9

int('2023-09'[-2:])
9
f = lambda x: int(x[-2:])
f('2023-09')
9
f('2022-10')
10

- 예시4: ‘2023-09’ \(\to\) 2023,9

f = lambda x: (int(x[:4]),int(x[-2:]))
f('2023-09')
(2023, 9)

- 예시5: 문자열이 ‘cat’이면 1 ’dog’ 이면 0

f = lambda x: 1 if x=='cat' else 0 
f('cat'),f('dog')
(1, 0)
